Odin Recipes: Roasted Carrots

Description

Always gotta have roasted carrots.

Ingredients

  • Carrots (duh)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive Oil

Prepare

  1. Wash carrots. Cut off the butt, and peel.
  2. Cut carrots diagonally.
  3. Spread them out on a cooking sheet with olive oil.
  4. Generously season with salt and pepper.
  5. Roast in the oven at 425 degrees Fahrenheit for 45 minutes.
